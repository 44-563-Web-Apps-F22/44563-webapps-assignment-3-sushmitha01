Yonkers Yak

yak, is a long-haired, short-legged oxlike mammal that was probably domesticated in Tibet but has been introduced wherever there are people at elevations of 4,000 to 6,000 metres, mainly in China but also in Central Asia, Mongolia, and Nepal.

  1. Yaks usually give birth in June
  2. Yaks live at the highest altitude of any mammal
  3. yak has more than one stomach
